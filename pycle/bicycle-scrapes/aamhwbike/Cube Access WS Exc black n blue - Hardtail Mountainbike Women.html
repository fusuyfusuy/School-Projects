Your "Cube Access WS Exc" introduces itself:

Combining comfort and performance in just the right blend, the Access WS Exc is your passport to ride-anywhere adventure. Featuring Shimano hydraulic disc brakes and a wide-ranging, slick-shifting Shimano transmission, it puts all-weather stopping power and fabulous hill-climbing ability at your fingertips. And the Suntour suspension fork - which improves comfort and control on rough surfaces, wherever you ride - even features a remote lockout, so you can enjoy bob-free climbing. Details, see?

Frame and equipment

Built around our Compact Agile Ride Geometry, the Access frame is designed to offer the best possible fit for day-long ride comfort. Our Size Split system makes it easy for every woman to select the perfect fit, too. A slightly lowered top tube improves confidence by adding extra standover clearance, and you can rest assured that the frame exceeds the tough safety standards set by our in-house testing lab.

For whom is the Cube Access WS Exc suitable?

For the sporty driver, who doesn't want to do without anything. Ladies mountain bikes have been neglected and smiled at for a long time. For some years now, the manufacturers have been offering specially produced series. The ever-increasing number of female users quickly made it a success to adapt the bikes especially to the needs of women. The male colleagues in weight and equipment in nothing following, these bikes offer also the suitable optics for each rider. No matter whether on the after-hours round, the weekend tour or the sporty trail - due to the wide selection you will find the right bike for every rider.

The advantages of 27.5 inch tires

27.5 inch wheels are the logical addition to the large 29 inch wheels and the optimum compromise for all smaller riders and trail fanatics. The combination of smoothness and manoeuvrability meets every wish of a biker. More agile than the 29 inch wheels and therefore the optimal choice for off-road driving and hairpin bends that leave you with little reaction time. This category offers the best choice especially for those changing from 26 inch wheels. 27.5 inch combines the best of two worlds and creates bikes that are not restricted in their use. Whether Fully or Hardtail - 27.5 inch is the golden mean and the perfect tyre size for all who want to use their mountain bike according to its purpose.

The advantages of 29 inch tires

29 inch is THE trend of the last few years, which was able to assert itself completely. Initially viewed critically by mountain bike purists and equipped with some teething troubles, the bike industry has recognized the trend and made it the new standard through continuous improvements. The advantage of the 29" tyres is the enormous running smoothness, which brings great advantages and saves power on long tours as well as uphill and downhill. Obstacles are easier to overcome due to the larger circumference of the wheels, and a greater distance is covered by turning the bottom bracket. By improvements and adjustments in the frame construction and the component selection, initial problems with the agility in the Trail and the optics of smaller frames were optimally balanced. Whether fully or hardtail, a 29 will take you far away.
