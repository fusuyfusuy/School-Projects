    Blank Digit BMX Bike

    Perfect for the Mini Shredders, the Blank Digit rolls upon 14" wheels and features all the great performance benefits of its bigger brothers. With its Ahead stem, Cassette hub, 25/10 gearing and Wanda 14" tyres, this bike is ready for riding freestyle anywhere.

    Features:

    • Frame: Blank 1020 Hi Ten, 14" Top Tube
    • Forks: Blank Compound 14" Hi Ten Forks
    • Brake: Rear Caliper Brake
    • Cable: Slic Cable
    • Brake Levers: Mini Levers
    • Chainwheel: 25t Sprocket Steel
    • Freewheel: 10t
    • Chain: S410
    • Headset: 1-1/8" External
    • Crank: Blank 1pc Hot Forged 127mm
    • Bottom Bracket: USA Open Bearing
    • Rims: 14" 20 Hole Alloy
    • Front Hub: 10mm Open Bearing Steel
    • Rear Hub: 10t 10mm Open Bearing Cassette
    • Tyres: Wanda 14" X 2.0" Steel Bead Tyre
    • Seat: Blank Combo Junior Seat
    • Handlebars: Blank Compound Mini 5.53" Rise
    • Handlebar Stem: Blank 1-1/8" Ahead 40mm Short Reach Stem
    • Grips: Mini Grips
    • Pedal: Mini' Plastic Freestyle Pedals

    Please note: this bike is for use on BMX tracks and skate parks. In many countries, it is illegal to use this bike on public roads without using the two independent braking systems provided (a front and rear brake).
