How To Use Pycle

1Fill The Form
We need your budget range and your height to find the best value bicycle. It will also fit your size!
2Answer The Questions
You will need to answer our questions for us to determine which type of bicycle would be best for you.
3Prioritize Criterias
This part is important! You need to prioritize parts or features of your dream bicyle. Use sliders to prioritize between use cases, and fill the table with your preference scores.






Please slide the slider to the side which you prefer.

Only move the slider if you are sure that you prefer one option to the other.

If you are not absolutely sure you prefer one to the other, please do not slide to the end, try to choose a middle point.

Be careful to be consistent.
